FacePlace

FacePlace is a full stack web application built from the ground up over the course of 9 days. It is designed to be reminicient of Facebook.

What can you do?

Start by creating an account. If you are short on time, try the demo login. It will log you in as Homer Simpson! Whichever way you choose, you will be intruduced to your news feed.

News feed look too empty? Thats because you don't have any friends yet! Try searching for your friends using the search bar. You can search by first name, last name or full name.

Add friends by clicking the corresponding button on the search results. Not sure if it is the right person? Visit their page first and add from there! While your there check out their profile page.

You just realized you have nothing in your own profile page. Simply click your name on the nav bar and you can go there in a flash. Clich the profile picture to upload a picture of yourself. Same goes for the cover photo button

Add information about yourself by clicking the about tab. You can add as much about youself as you like under presorted catagories.
