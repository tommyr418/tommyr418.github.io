About Me

I am a Full Stack Software developer. This is my story.

I graduated college with a B.S. in Biomedical Sciences. I originally had the goal of going to medical school because I loved diagnosing problems and finding the solution or cure for them. After getting some first hand experience in the healthcare field, I decided it was not for me because of how clerical the work had become and the poor work life balance. I wanted to take my critical thinking skills and find a path that allowed me the thrive.

Then I found programming. As soon as I learned to print "hello world", I immediately fell in love with programming. It was a perfect match for my analytical mind. Figuring out something new and implementing it was the most satisfying feeling I had in a long time. I knew that if I became a developer, I would never find it dull and would be challenged in ways I had never been prior. That just made me more excited.

After many hours of hard work and studying, I decided to accelerate my progress by attending a very intense coding bootcamp, App Academy. The work was hard, often 100 hours a week, but I pulled through and succeeded in acquiring the skills to be a full stack developer.
