Background

I graduated college with a B.S. in Biomedical sciences. Originally with the goal of going to medical school, I worked in the healthcare industry for 2 years as an assistant to a gastroenterologist. I learned a lot about the day to day of a physician. I loved the deductiive reasoning that doctors had to employ to reach a diagnosis and propose a treatment. However, I also saw the negative impact of the heathcare industry as a whole on the life of physicians. I decided to take my love of problem solving in on a different path.
