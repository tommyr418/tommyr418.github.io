Chance Quest

Chance Quest is an exciting turn based strategy game with elements inspired by the board game Risk.

How do you play?

Each player starts the game with one territory which is also the base. Players take turns moving units around to take over more territories to increase the prestige of their kingdom. Eventually players will need to compete for territories through battle. Gain then advantage of more resources to overwehelm your opponents!
